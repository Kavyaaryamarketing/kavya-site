You can't claim growth without a starting line. So before any experiments, here's the honest baseline.

## Where things stand today

- **Subscribers:** the people I personally told.
- **Traffic:** essentially zero — the site just went live.
- **Indexed pages:** submitting the sitemap to Search Console today.

## The instrumentation

Before chasing traffic, I set up the measurement so I'm never guessing:

- Google Analytics 4 for traffic and behaviour
- Google Search Console for what I rank for
- Newsletter analytics for open and click rates
- A simple UTM convention so every link is attributable

## The hypothesis I'm testing first

That consistent, genuinely useful writing plus basic SEO hygiene will, on its own, produce a slow but real climb in organic traffic over 90 days — before I spend a rupee on ads.

Next month: the first real numbers, and whether that held up.
